About Darakson

Darakson is a project that aims to develop an iOS application for children who are interested in simple robotics, giving them a platform where they can create their own account in order to use the application, personalize their profile, control the movement of the physical car built using raspberry pi 3 (model b+ ) for short and long distances, and having a live stream of the car from its attached camera.
